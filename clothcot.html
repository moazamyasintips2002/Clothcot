Our Story

Made in Faisalabad, worn everywhere.

Clothcot was started by a small team of tailors and designers in Faisalabad — Pakistan's textile city — who believed the shalwar kameez deserved the same attention to fit and finish as any Western suit.

Every fabric is sourced from local mills, every embroidery pattern hand-drawn before it reaches a needle, and every pant-shirt cut to sit right whether you're at a desk or a dawat.
